WHAT ARE THE NIGHTCLUB'S OPENING HOURS?

We are open from 8PM to 4AM, Friday through Sunday.

What is the Age Limit?

All guests must be 18 years of age or older (strict).

What is the dress code?

Fashionable upscale attire required.

What is an Acceptable Form of Identification?

A current, non-expired, valid photo ID is required of every patron visiting our Nightclub and all guests must be 18 years of age or older. We will also accept foreign ID’s and passports if they include a photo ID, have a name and date of birth in English, and can be validated as a real ID.


We do not accept expired IDs, punched IDs, birth certificates or photocopies of IDs. False and/or counterfeit IDs will be confiscated and turned over to the local police department. PlanetG Nightclub reserves the right to refuse entry to anyone.

Do I need to make a reservation?

Reservations are not required but are highly recommended, especially on weekends.

Articles Prohibited?

Athletic gear and apparel T-shirts, bulky plaid, shorts, torn or cutoff clothing, men’s sandals, sneakers, athletic shoes, jordans, hats, baggy clothing, chains, offensive prints on clothing, weapons, controlled substances, eye drops, gum, outside food or beverages, liquids, vitamins, supplements, over-the-counter medications, prescription medications without valid prescription, cameras / go pros, smart glasses, wearable technology, selfie sticks, the use of vaporizers “vapes” any device found in use will be confiscated and disposed of by management without compensation. Guests noticeably intoxicated or under the influence of a controlled substance will be denied entry and will not be eligible to receive a refund, all persons, bags & personal items are subject to search, if you remove your shoes you will immediately be escorted out of the venue. We have a zero tolerance policy.

What Type of Music do You Play?

We will offer a variety of musical genres depending on the night featuring open format, top 40’s, EDM, top latin music, reggaeton, upbeat clean hiphop, top 40s, mash-ups and EDM. Please check the upcoming events page for specific artists and events.

Am I Allowed to Bring in a Bag?

Any oversized bags, backpacks, purses, or parcels are not allowed. All items are subject to search. ​

How Can I Be Featured as an Upcoming DJ?

We’ll contact you if interested. Thanks!​​ ​

How Do I Cure My Hangover?

Drink plenty of Water and Rest. ​

Do You Have a Lost & Found?

We do a thorough search of our venue upon closing. Please contact our management at 949-691-8278​​ daily from 12PM-10PM, as they can assist with confirming if your lost item was found. If your item was found, we will arrange a pick-up time. Typically, this is the same as our operating hours.​ ​
